Doorbell

A smart doorbell is a high-tech replacement for traditional doorbells. Equipped with a camera and microphone, it allows homeowners to see and communicate with visitors remotely via a smartphone app. Some models also offer motion detection and video recording features, enhancing home security and convenience.


Home Hub

A home hub serves as the central control system for various smart devices in a household. It connects and manages devices like smart lights, thermostats, and speakers, allowing users to control them from a single interface.


Irrigation system

A smart irrigation system optimizes outdoor water usage by incorporating sensors and weather data. It automatically adjusts watering schedules based on real-time weather conditions, soil moisture levels, and plant needs.


Lighting system

Smart lighting systems enable users to control the brightness, color, and ambiance of lights in their home. These systems often work with voice assistants or mobile apps, allowing users to create custom lighting scenes, set schedules, and adjust lighting remotely. Smart lighting enhances energy efficiency and provides personalized lighting experiences.


Security Camera

Smart security cameras offer real-time video surveillance and recording capabilities. They are equipped with motion detection, night vision, and sometimes facial recognition features. Users can access live feeds and recorded footage remotely through smartphone apps, enhancing home security by allowing homeowners to monitor their property from anywhere.


Smart Blinds

Smart blinds are motorized window coverings that can be controlled remotely. Users can adjust the blinds' position, tilt, or schedule them to open and close at specific times.


Smart Lock

A smart lock is an innovative home security device that replaces traditional locks, offering advanced features and remote control capabilities. These locks can be controlled through a smartphone app, allowing homeowners to lock or unlock their doors from anywhere.


Smart plug

Smart plugs are small devices that can transform ordinary appliances and devices into smart, connected gadgets. By simply plugging them into existing outlets, smart plugs enable remote control and automation through smartphone apps or voice assistants.


Smart speaker

A smart speaker is a voice-activated device equipped with virtual assistants like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, or Apple's Siri. These devices offer a wide range of functionalities, from playing music and providing weather updates to answering questions and controlling other smart home devices.


Smart TV

A smart TV combines traditional television functions with internet connectivity and interactive features. These TVs allow users to stream content from popular services like Netflix, Hulu, and YouTube directly on the television screen, eliminating the need for additional streaming devices.
